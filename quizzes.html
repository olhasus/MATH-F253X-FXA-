Quizzes

Blank Quizzes will be available on Gradescope.

Students will need to complete and upload them between 5:00 PM and 12:00 PM on a due date.

You will have 45 minutes during that time to download the quiz, write your answers to the quiz, and upload the quiz into Gradescope.

  • If you encounter technical difficulties or there’s an issue with timing, you need to immediately email your instructor with details and include a scan of your quiz (if possible) in the email.

  • If you are writing your quiz on paper, you will need to scan or photoscan (using a scanning app on your smartphone) and convert it to a PDF file, or save your work as a PDF if you are working on a computer or tablet.

    • Links to this semester’s quizzes, and solutions, will appear here as they are completed.
